The Human Fund: Money for People

Our Mission

At The Human Fund, our mission is to dedicate ourselves to the noble cause of redistributing money to humans in need, ensuring everyone gets a breath of financial fresh air.

How You Can Help

  • Dream Teleposition: Before going to sleep, imagine a donation of your desired amount. When you awake, you'll find that your donation has been transferred to our offices via the Dream Realm

  • Quantum Contribution: Through the power of quantum entanglement, your donations may or may not be instantly transmitted across space and time.
